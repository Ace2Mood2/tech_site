Products

Connected Home Wired

The wired solution involves laying cables in a rough-finished flat, i.e. before renovation.

IP - integration possibilities with video intercoms

Connect to your camera from anywhere in the world and always be aware of what's going on in your home.

ElGate - Smart Barrier, functionality and features

Smart Barrier is the perfect solution for car park areas. Number plate reading by a camera and control of the barrier via a mobile app will greatly simplify and speed up the passage process

Connected Home Wireless

The wireless solution has the same functionality as the wired solution. The ease of installation means that low-current cabling is not required, which is a great solution for renovation projects.

Smart Locks

Smart lock - a secure and convenient way to open your door. Fingerprint, PIN, RF card - choose one or combine several options and create the best one that suits your needs. A mechanical key and emergency power connector are provided for backup opening.
